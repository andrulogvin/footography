Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2026

1. Our Data Philosophy

Footography GO is designed to be a private sneaker vault. Most of your data, including photos of your sneakers and collection details, are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your personal images to our servers.

2. Information We Collect

To provide a premium experience, we collect minimal data:

  • Profile Metadata: Locally stored username and avatar.
  • Operational Analytics: Anonymous data to optimize app performance.
  • Device Metrics: Technical specs to ensure features work flawlessly on your hardware.

3. AI Insights & Processing

Our app uses secure AI services to provide sneaker facts. No personal data or photos are shared; we only transmit the brand and model name to fetch historical insights.
